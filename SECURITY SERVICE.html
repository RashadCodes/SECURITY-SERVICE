ministry of interior

The Ministry of the Interior is a ministry of the government of Ghana mandated to ensure internal security, as well as the maintenance of law and order in the country

the ghana police service


this is ghana police

The Ghana Police Service is one of the Public Sector Organisations (PSOs) under the Ministry of the Interior. The Ghana Police Service was created by the colonial administration to produce an enabling environment for commerce through the maintenance of law and order as well as the arrest and prosecution of people who fall foul of the laws


ghana prison service


this is prisons

The Ghana Prisons Service, as an agent operating under the ambit of the Ministry of the Interior, contributes to the maintenance of public safety by ensuring the safe custody of criminals convicted by the courts to terms of imprisonment.


Ghana immigration service


this is immigration

The Ghana Immigration Service has been established as the Agency under the Ministry of the Interior to advise on and to ensure the effective implementation of all laws and regulations pertaining to immigration and related issues.
